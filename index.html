About

TECHXTER 13.0 is a National Level Technical Paper Presentation, open to all the branches of engineering. This event is specially designed in such a way that it tests all aspects of the students' knowledge on varied topics. It provides the participants an excellent opportunity for demonstrating technical competence and presentation skills and innovative ideas with Emerging Technologies. Students can use this event as a platform to showcase different and upcoming technologies. TECHXTER 13.0 would be held on March 22, 2024. We are thrilled to celebrate the anniversary of TECHXTER with its 13th Iteration. TECHXTER has embraced the Theme "Sustainable Development", to emphasize and understand the importance of technology, and to highlight the influence of Technical advancements in different fields.
